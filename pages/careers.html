Executive Chef

Harbor District Location

Full-time

Lead our culinary team in creating exceptional seafood dishes. Oversee menu development, kitchen operations, and staff training while maintaining our high standards of quality and innovation.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of executive chef experience in fine dining
  • Expertise in seafood preparation and Mediterranean cuisine
  • Strong leadership and team management skills
  • Culinary degree or equivalent experience

Sous Chef

Marina Bay Location

Full-time

Support the executive chef in daily kitchen operations, menu execution, and staff supervision. Help maintain our commitment to fresh, sustainable seafood preparation.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of sous chef or senior line cook experience
  • Knowledge of seafood handling and preparation
  • Ability to work in fast-paced environment
  • Strong organizational and communication skills

Server

All Locations

Full-time / Part-time

Provide exceptional dining experiences for our guests. Share your knowledge of our seafood offerings and wine pairings while delivering attentive, professional service.

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of fine dining service experience
  • Knowledge of wine and seafood pairings preferred
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work evenings and weekends

Bartender

Downtown Location

Full-time

Craft exceptional cocktails and manage our extensive wine and spirits program. Create memorable experiences for guests at our bar and throughout the restaurant.

  • Extensive knowledge of wines, spirits, and cocktails
  • Flair for creativity and presentation
  • Strong multitasking abilities

Host/Hostess
